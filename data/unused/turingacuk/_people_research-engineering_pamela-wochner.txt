Pamela Wochner is a Research Data Scientist at The Alan Turing Institute. She holds a MSc in electrical engineering from the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology. Prior to joining the Turing, she worked on a PhD in medical imaging at King’s College London, where she focused on the development of a novel acquisition method for diffusion magnetic resonance imaging. 
 
At the Turing, Pamela focuses on applying and extending her experience in data analysis, simulation and machine learning to projects that tackle real-world problems.
